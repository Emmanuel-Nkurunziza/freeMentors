As a Mentor you will be able to see all learnes (users) who have requested you to be their mentor!
You can schedule to offer mentorship sessions as requested. if you click accept, a message will be sent to the learner to inform him/her that he/she has been granted a mentorship session. And this will make the mentorship session page available for the learner in order for him/her ask a you (a) question(s) he want to be mentored to!
Otherwise you can decline to the requested mentorship session.
if you choose to decline, it will be helpful to leave a note explaining the raison why a mentorship session has been declined.

We thank you very much for your contribution to the community!
